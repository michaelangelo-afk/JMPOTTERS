Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard under "My Orders."
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unworn, unused items in their original packaging. Footwear must be tried on indoors only. Contact us to initiate a return.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery within Lagos typically takes 1-3 business days. For interstate orders (across Nigeria), delivery takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we only ship within Nigeria. We're working on expanding internationally — stay tuned!
